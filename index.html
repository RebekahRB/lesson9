Do you want to make arrows fly like Katniss Everdeen of The Hunger Games or Oliver Queen of Arrow?

Straight to the Point Archery is where you can start! My name is Giap Nguyen and I started Straight to the Point Archery in 2005. I'm an avid and accomplished archer having competed with the United States Archery Team in the 2000 Summer Olympics. I started Straight to the Point in part because I was not satisfied with the archery eduction options for young people in Tacoma. After The Hunger Games books and movies came out, I noticed a surge in the popularity of archery among young people, especially young women. I founded Straigh to the Point because I wanted to provide a quality environment for learning this fun sport.
